# Global Commodity Markets Analysis (2000–2026)

## About This Project
I built this project to understand how global commodity prices have 
moved over the last 25 years. As an MBA student specialising in 
Finance and Business Analytics, I wanted to go beyond textbook 
theory and work with real market data.

The dataset covers 6 commodities — Gold, Silver, Platinum, 
Crude Oil WTI, Copper, and Natural Gas — from January 2000 
to June 2026.

## Live Dashboard
[Click here to view the interactive Tableau dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/shared/JM7YXJWB9)

## What I Found
- Gold has been the strongest performer, surging sharply after 2020
- Platinum was highly volatile during the 2008 financial crisis
- COVID-19 in 2020 caused a clear spike in price volatility
- Natural Gas and Copper stayed relatively flat over the entire period

## How I Built It
- Downloaded commodity price data from Kaggle
- Cleaned and prepared the data in Microsoft Excel
- Added helper columns for monthly grouping and price range
- Built an interactive dashboard in Tableau Public
